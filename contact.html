Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my café to CaffyRute?

We're currently building our café database manually. If you're a café owner, please contact us with your café details and we'll add it to our platform. In the future, we'll have a self-service portal for café owners.

Is CaffyRute available as a mobile app?

We're currently working on mobile applications for both iOS and Android. For now, our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. Follow us on social media for updates on the mobile app release.

How accurate is the location data?

We use Google Maps API to provide accurate location data and real-time information about café locations, hours, and directions. Our data is regularly updated to ensure accuracy.

Can I leave reviews for cafés?

The review system is currently in development. Soon you'll be able to rate cafés, leave detailed reviews, and upload photos to help other coffee lovers make informed decisions.

Do you charge cafés to be listed?

Basic listings on CaffyRute are completely free for café owners. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core mission is to help people discover great cafés regardless of budget.

How can I report incorrect information?

If you notice incorrect information about a café, please contact us using the form above or email us directly. We appreciate community feedback to keep our data accurate and up-to-date.
